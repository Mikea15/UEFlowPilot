## Creating a new FlowPilot Asset

<Steps>

1. In your Content Browser, under `Gameplay` menu there's a `FlowPilot` menu.
    <Image src={create_flowpilot_asset} alt="create_flowpilot_asset" />

2. You can now rename this FlowPilot to whatever you'd like.

3. Opening the Asset will open up the custom editor.
    <Image src={new_asset_view} alt="new_asset_view" />

3. You're ready to go

</Steps>
